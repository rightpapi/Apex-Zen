# ApexPredator Values

These values are intended for use with the ApexPredator script.

They have been tested to provide a solid baseline for recoil compensation, but they are not guaranteed to match every player's setup. Controller settings, sensitivity, deadzones, hardware, and personal preference can all affect performance, so you may need to make small adjustments to achieve the best results.

Please remember that these values are **not designed to make your aim perfect** or completely remove recoil. They are intended to provide the closest possible recoil compensation while still requiring proper tracking and mechanical skill.

## SMGs

Volt: V -6, H 10 | Hipfire 4

RE-45: V 0, H 0 | Hipfire 0
